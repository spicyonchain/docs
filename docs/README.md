## Introduction

**spicy** is a decentralized, blockchain-powered content-sharing platform designed to empower creators and users alike. It combines the principles of Web3 with an innovative token-based economy, creating a space where creators can monetize their work and users can access exclusive content seamlessly while earning rewards for their engagement.

---

## Key Features

- **Decentralized Hosting**: Content is stored on IPFS, ensuring transparency, security, and resistance to censorship. This decentralized approach guarantees that content remains accessible and tamper-proof.
- **Monetization Options**: Creators have the flexibility to choose from various monetization models, including free access, subscription-based content, and pay-per-view. This allows creators to tailor their offerings to different audience segments.
- **$SPICY Token**: The platform's native cryptocurrency, $SPICY, is used for all transactions, providing a trustless and transparent financial system. Users can earn tokens through engagement and staking, fostering an active community.
- **Access Control**: Smart contracts govern content access, allowing creators to set specific terms for content availability. This ensures that only authorized users can access premium content.
- **Low Fees**: A competitive 5% platform fee ensures that creators retain the majority of their earnings, while also supporting the platform's growth and sustainability.

---

## Referral System

The **spicy** platform includes a robust referral system designed to incentivize users to invite others to join the platform. This system rewards users for each successful referral, enhancing community growth and engagement.

### How It Works

- **Referral Process**: You can share your wallet address with new users. When a new user enters your address as their referral during registration, you earn points.
- **Earning Points**: 
  - **First 10 Referrals**: Earn 10,000 points for each of the first 10 referrals.
  - **Next 100 Referrals**: Earn 1,000 points for each referral.
  - **Subsequent Referrals**: Earn 500 points for each additional referral.
- **Verification**: Referrals are verified and recorded on-chain through our smart contract, ensuring transparency and immutability.

### Benefits of the Referral System

- **Community Growth**: Encourages users to invite others, expanding the platform's user base.
- **Rewards**: Provides tangible benefits to users who actively participate in growing the community.
- **Transparency**: All referral activities are recorded on-chain, ensuring fairness and transparency.

### How to Participate

1. **Get started**: Become a user of the platform by entering a referral address.
2. **Share Your Address**: Share your wallet address with potential new users.
3. **Earn Points**: When new users register using your address, you earn points based on the referral structure.
4. **Track Your Progress**: View your total referrals and points in the referral section of your account.

### Using Referral Points

- **Platform Benefits**: Use points to access exclusive content and features on the platform.
- **Future Rewards**: Points may be redeemable for additional rewards or benefits as the platform evolves.

---

This referral system is a key component of the **spicy** platform's strategy to foster a vibrant and engaged community. By participating, users not only earn rewards but also contribute to the platform's growth and success.

## $SPICY Tokenomics

### Total Supply

- **Total Supply**: 69,000,000 $SPICY tokens (fixed supply, non-mintable)
- **Token Type**: ERC-20 
- **Decimals**: 18

### Token Allocation

1. **Community Rewards (45%) - 31,050,000 $SPICY**
   - **Purpose**: Incentivize user engagement, content creation, and staking.
   - **Usage**:
     - Engagement rewards for creators and users.
     - Staking rewards to encourage token locking and platform loyalty.
   - **Emission Schedule**: Linear distribution over **5 years**, starting with higher rewards initially.

2. **Presale and Liquidity (26%) - 17,940,000 $SPICY**
   - **Purpose**: Fund platform launch and provide liquidity for trading.
   - **Breakdown**:
     - **Presale**: Raise initial funds to support development and operations.
     - **Liquidity**: Establish liquidity pools on decentralized exchanges.
   - **Sale Terms**: Presale token price set at fair market value. No vesting for liquidity tokens; locked liquidity for **2 years**.

3. **Treasury and Development (19%) - 13,110,000 $SPICY**
   - **Purpose**: Ensure platform growth, ecosystem development, and long-term sustainability.
   - **Usage**:
     - Feature development, infrastructure scaling, and marketing campaigns.
     - Reserved for grants, bounties, and partnerships to grow the ecosystem.
   - **Vesting**: Unlock over **4 years** with a **6-month cliff**.

4. **Team and Advisors (10%) - 6,900,000 $SPICY**
   - **Purpose**: Reward the founding team and advisors for building and scaling the platform.
   - **Vesting**: Vesting over **4 years** with a **12-month cliff** to align incentives with long-term success.

### Utility of $SPICY Tokens

1. **Content Monetization**: Used by users to pay for access to exclusive content (subscriptions, pay-per-view).
2. **Rewards**: Earned by creators and users for engagement and staking.
3. **Staking**: Lock tokens to earn rewards and unlock premium features.
4. **Governance**: Token holders vote on platform upgrades and key decisions.

## Platform Workflow

- **Content Upload**: Creators can easily upload content to the platform, which is then stored on IPFS and managed via smart contracts. This process ensures that content is secure and accessible.
- **Access and Payments**: Users can access content by paying with $SPICY tokens. Smart contracts ensure that transactions are secure and that access is granted only to authorized users.
- **Rewards**: Users earn rewards for engaging with content, providing feedback, and participating in community activities. This fosters a vibrant and active community.

---

## Decentralized Storage

- **IPFS Utilization**: Spicy leverages the InterPlanetary File System (IPFS) for decentralized content storage. This ensures that all content is distributed across a network of nodes, enhancing security and availability.
- **Smart Contracts**: These are deployed on the Ethereum blockchain to manage content access, payments, and rewards. They automate processes and ensure that transactions are secure and transparent.
- **User Interfaces**: Built with React and Next.js, the platform offers a seamless and responsive user experience across devices, ensuring accessibility and ease of use.

---

## Smart Contracts

- **Access Management**: Smart contracts automate the process of granting access to content based on user payments or subscriptions. This reduces the need for manual intervention and enhances security.
- **Payment Processing**: All financial transactions are conducted on-chain using $SPICY tokens. This ensures that payments are secure, transparent, and immutable.
- **Staking**: Users can stake $SPICY tokens to earn rewards, contributing to the platform's security and liquidity. This incentivizes users to hold and use tokens within the ecosystem.
